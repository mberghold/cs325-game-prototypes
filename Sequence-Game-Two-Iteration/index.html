Spy Game

  • High Concept: Find the spy that has sneaked in.
  • Theme: Used my own theme.
  • Mandated Variety: Clicking, completely random, mystery.
  • Player Experience Goals: Players will have fun trying to find the spy.
  • Gameplay: The player manages a base of operations. There are nine agents represented by houses. By clicking on a house, you switch the light and chimney of that house and the four adjacent. When a house is occupied, the agent who lives in the house is considered in the base. If the enemy spy is in the base, there is a 50% chance that he will hack in. Using who's on base, the player must deduce who the enemy spy is.
  • Prototype Goal: Random logic puzzle.
  • Features: Unique game idea.
  • Setting/Premise: We have agents doing some great work here. However, there is an enemy spy that has been hacking us. We must determine who this spy is.
  • Story: There is no real story to this.
  • Target Audience: Everyone.
  • Play Time: About two minutes.
  • Strategies: You want to keep the spy hacking as long as possible, because that's when you can control whether or not you can see him.
