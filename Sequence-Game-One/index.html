What's Your Deal?

  • High Concept: Player and computer pick card values to win turn by turn.
  • Theme: I think I made my own theme.
  • Mandated Variety: Mouse input and one key on the keyboard, Part deterministic, part random, card game
  • Player Experience Goals: Players should have an engaging time determining what is the optimal cards to play at any given time.
  • Gameplay: The core mechanic is choosing a card. This is done by clicking on the cards on the screen. You can press I for instructions.
  • Prototype Goal: The card vs card gameplay feature.
  • Features: I think it's never been made before, at least to my knowledge. It's kind of like war but with some strategy.
  • Setting/Premise: You are in a card game.
  • Story: No story really.
  • Target Audience: I think anyone could enjoy playing this.
  • Play Time: Less than five minutes.
  • Strategies: I'm not really sure yet, I've tried a lot of different things but I haven't found any strategies yet. For example, I dont know if picking into ties are optimal or not.
